Built from the Ground Up

EMG was born in the field, where experience meets innovation. Recognizing gaps in safety, efficiency, and specialized service within the mining sector, our founder Jake Tullock set out to build a company that could bridge those gaps with cutting-edge technologies like patent-pending High Reach Resin Bolting and robotic shotcrete systems. From underground tunnels to open-pit quarries, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for mining and geotechnical solutions.
