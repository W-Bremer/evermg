Frequently Asked Questions

What types of mining operations does Everett Mining & Geotechnical support?



Everett Mining & Geotechnical supports underground, open-pit, and quarry mining operations, delivering specialized services like ground stabilization, drilling, and development for projects of any scale.

How does EMG ensure safety in underground mining projects?



EMG follows rigorous safety protocols, employs ASA Certified Shotcrete Nozzlemen, and uses advanced technologies like robotic shotcrete and high-reach bolting to minimize risk and ensure operational safety.

What is High Reach Resin Bolting, and why is it important?



Our patent-pending High Reach Resin Bolting system allows secure anchoring at heights over 105 feet, improving safety and efficiency in hard-to-reach areas of underground mines.

How quickly can EMG mobilize for a new project?



We maintain rapid mobilization capabilities to meet project deadlines efficiently. Depending on project size and scope, we can often deploy teams within two weeks.

Do you offer custom solutions for challenging geotechnical conditions?



Yes, EMG specializes in tailored solutions. We collaborate with a diverse network of specialist engineering firms to address unique site challenges, from water management to complex slope stabilization.

Hand and Machine Scale

Removal of loose rock and debris from rock faces and tunnel surfaces using both manual (hand scaling) and mechanical (machine scaling) methods to ensure safety.

Underground Directional Core Drilling

Specialized drilling technique used underground to obtain core samples from precisely targeted geological zones by controlling the drill hole trajectory.

Micropiles

High-capacity, small-diameter drilled and grouted piles used for foundation support, underpinning existing structures, and slope stabilization, especially in difficult ground or access conditions.

What services do you offer?

We provide a range of mining and geotechnical services including site assessments, project management, and compliance consulting. Our team is dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions tailored to your specific needs. Explore our services page for more details.

How can I request a consultation?

To request a consultation, simply fill out our online form or give us a call. Our team will get back to you promptly to discuss your project needs. We look forward to assisting you!

What are your hours?

Our office hours are Monday through Friday, from 8 AM to 5 PM. We are closed on weekends and major holidays. Feel free to reach out during business hours for assistance.

We are based in the Ozarks, serving clients throughout the region. Our location allows us to efficiently manage projects and respond to client needs. Visit our locations page for more detailed information.
